Most minor component replacements, maintenance runs, or quick diagnoses (like fan cleaning or baseline power supply checks) take between 1 to 2 days. Complex television panel or multi-layered audio system troubleshooting can take 3 to 5 business days depending on component availability.

Yes, we offer a standard repair warranty on all replaced electronic components. This covers any issues relating specifically to the original fault repaired; conditions do not cover subsequent external power surges, physical impacts, or liquid damage.

We provide home diagnostic services and inspections for large household devices like heavy home theater configurations and mounted smart TVs. Smaller components or portable items are best brought straight to our workshop for precision diagnostic testing and bench work.
